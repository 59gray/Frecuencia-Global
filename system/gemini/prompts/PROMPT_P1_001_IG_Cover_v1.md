**Sistema:** Frecuencia Global / Gemini Visual Creator

**Dimensiones:** 1080×1350 (4:5)

## Brand Constraints

- Fondo base `#0A0A0F`
- Acento dominante `#00E5FF` (Geopolitik Drop)
- Acento secundario `#FF00E5` en proporción menor (≤20%)
- Look dark editorial, institucional, contemporáneo
- ADN electrónico sutil, no festival ni sci-fi genérico
- Sin texto embebido (se añade en post-producción con Pillow/Canva)
- Componentes permitidos: cables submarinos, mapas de rutas oceánicas, nodos de red, grids sutiles, data overlays, fondos marinos abstractos
- Componentes prohibidos: globo genérico, audífonos, tornamesas, bocinas, equalizers, estética rave/festival

## Primary Prompt

```text
Generate a vertical editorial cover image (4:5 aspect ratio) for an Instagram post about submarine internet cables for "Frecuencia Global", a geopolitics media brand.

COMPOSITION: vertical 4:5 portrait orientation, visual weight centered with breathing room at top and bottom for text overlay zones, layered depth suggesting ocean floor, asymmetrical focal point slightly right of center.
STYLE: Frecuencia Global dark editorial, institutional, contemporary, subtle electronic DNA, serious and premium, geopolitical analysis aesthetic.
LIGHTING: deep underwater ambient lighting, electric cyan (#00E5FF) as dominant light source emanating from cable routes, faint magenta (#FF00E5) reflections in deep water, low-key dramatic contrast.
COLOR: base #0A0A0F deep black transitioning to deep ocean blue-black, dominant accent #00E5FF electric cyan on cable paths and data nodes, restrained #FF00E5 magenta as secondary glow, no colors outside the Frecuencia Global palette.
TEXTURE: subtle ocean depth grain, thin topographic/bathymetric grid at very low opacity, smooth dark gradients, clean surface quality suggesting deep water pressure.
DEPTH: strong foreground-to-background layering, closest cable in sharp focus, distant network fading into deep ocean haze, atmospheric underwater perspective.
ELEMENTS ALLOWED: submarine fiber optic cables glowing cyan, ocean floor terrain, network route maps overlaid subtly, data flow particles along cables, connection nodes where cables meet land, abstract continental shelf outlines, subtle sonar/radar rings.
ELEMENTS PROHIBITED: text, logos, captions, fish, divers, ships, generic globe icon, headphones, turntables, speakers, equalizers, festival lights, cartoon style, bright colors outside palette.
ASPECT RATIO: 4:5 vertical (1080×1350 for Instagram).
LEGIBILITY PRIORITY: reserve top 25% and bottom 20% slightly darker/calmer for text overlay in post-production.
BRAND ALIGNMENT: Frecuencia Global — geopolitics platform analyzing critical global infrastructure; the image must feel like hidden power, deep infrastructure, and strategic vulnerability.

Create a polished, publication-ready cover image with no text embedded. The mood should evoke: "the invisible network that controls the world lies beneath the ocean."
```
